Remarks

Lensophy handle the life cycle of HttpClient through the IHttpClientFactory.

Therefore, avoid instantiating LensophyService directly, with a new HttpClient(). Consider register the dependency with AddLensophy(IServiceCollection, string) extension, unless you know how to use IHttpClientFactory properly.

For curiosity, even when wrapped in a using scope, you may not have control over when the HttpClient is disposed, potentially leading to more instances than the Garbage Collector can release, resulting in a SocketException issue.
